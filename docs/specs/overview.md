## About

A relaxed budgeting system that helps people forecast how much they can safely spend until payday, while letting them track as much or as little detail as they actually tolerate.

> “Help users feel financially aware without feeling financially controlled.”

## Target Users

- Casual: cares about safe to spend, bills, payday forecast
- Average: can use categories, spending labels and recurring tracking
- Power: detailed budgets, goals, analytics, manual adjustments
